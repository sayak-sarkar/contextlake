Brand

Brand overview

contextlake's brand in one page: essence, voice, the lake metaphor, Pebble, the palette, and the mark, with the full spec linked.

contextlake's brand in one page: who it is, how it sounds, and the marks and colors that carry it. This is the friendly tour. The full decision-grade spec, every hex, ratio, and construction rule, lives in BRANDING.md.

What contextlake is#

contextlake is the local context layer that lets your AI coding tools answer from your real source instead of their best guess. The category noun is always "context layer", never "tool", "platform", or "data lake". The name is always one lowercase word: contextlake.

Personality: grounded, lucid, warm#

Three words govern everything contextlake says.

  • Grounded. Concrete, cited claims. Calm authority, no hype. The promise is provenance, that an answer traces to real source, not a claim of being never wrong.
  • Lucid. Short sentences, plain words, one idea per line.
  • Warm. Second person, light human touches, friendly but never cutesy. Pebble is on your side.

How this sounds in practice is the job of the documentation style guide.

The lake metaphor#

One controlling metaphor: a clear lake seen in depth. Deep is the real, complete source underneath; clear is the precise answer back. The lake is calm and legible to the bottom, never threatening. No oceans, waves, fishing, or drowning, and never "data lake".

Taglines#

  • Master: "Deep context. Clear answers."
  • Descriptive: "All your real context, in one local lake."
  • Functional: "A local, offline-first context layer for AI coding tools."
  • Proof line: "Answers from real source."

Pebble#

Pebble is contextlake's mascot: a small, friendly, dark blue-grey river otter who surfaces from the lake cradling a glowing translucent context-pebble in both forepaws, offering it forward. The one-beat read is "I went down, I found the real thing, here it is." Calm, competent, never frantic. Pebble is the anti-hallucination promise made friendly.

Color#

Six locked brand primitives carry the palette. Deepwater and mist are the dark and light tones; lake and current are the two brand teals; shore and sun are warm accents (illustration and emphasis only).

The full color system (dark and light theme roles, WCAG-verified contrast ratios, the on-fill law, and gold discipline) is in the spec.

The mark and wordmark#

The logo is a dual-element identity: a flat, front-facing otter-head mark cradling the context-pebble over a deepwater keyline, plus the contextlake wordmark set in Space Grotesk Bold, lowercase, one color. The mark carries the brand at small sizes and as an avatar; the wordmark carries it in reading contexts.

The full spec#
